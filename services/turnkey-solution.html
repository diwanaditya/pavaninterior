What Is a Turnkey Interior Project?

A turnkey interior project means a single professional team handles the entire project from design, planning, procurement, and execution to final handover — so the client does not have to coordinate with multiple vendors or contractors.

At Pavan Interior Design, turnkey solutions offer complete peace of mind. We take responsibility for timelines, quality, materials, execution, and coordination — delivering a finished space that is ready to use.
