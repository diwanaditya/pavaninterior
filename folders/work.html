The People Behind Our Work

Every interior we create is shaped by the people behind it. Our team brings together thoughtful design, strong execution, and years of hands-on experience to deliver spaces that feel refined, functional, and enduring.

Pavan Balani

Founder

Pavan Balani is the founder of Pavan Interior Design and the guiding force behind the studio’s design philosophy. With over a decade of experience in the interior industry, he has led the execution of residential, commercial, and turnkey projects across India.

His approach is rooted in clarity, transparency, and attention to detail — ensuring that every project is thoughtfully planned, responsibly executed, and delivered exactly as envisioned.

Project Manager

Planning & Coordination

The project manager oversees the complete lifecycle of each project, from planning and vendor coordination to on-site execution. Their role ensures that timelines, budgets, and quality standards are consistently maintained.

By aligning design intent with execution on the ground, they help deliver smooth workflows and dependable outcomes across all project scales.

Site Supervisor

On-Site Execution

The site supervisor manages daily on-site operations, ensuring workmanship quality, material accuracy, and adherence to approved drawings.

Acting as the link between planning and execution, they ensure that every detail is implemented with care and every project progresses smoothly to completion.

We design and execute interiors with a focus on quality, transparency, and long-term value — across residential and commercial spaces.
