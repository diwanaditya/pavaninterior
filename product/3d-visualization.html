3D Interior Design & Visualization

Experience your space before it is built. Our advanced 3D interior design and visualization services help you see, modify, and perfect every detail before execution begins.

Why 3D Design Matters in Interior Projects

3D interior visualization is the foundation of accurate interior planning. It eliminates assumptions, reduces execution errors, and ensures complete alignment between client expectations and final output. At Pavan Interior Design, we specialize in hyper-realistic 3D designs that reflect real materials, lighting, proportions, and spatial flow.

Our 3D Design Process

01. Requirement Understanding

We study your layout, lifestyle, budget, and design preferences in detail before starting any visual work.

02. Space Planning & Concepts

Furniture layouts, zoning, circulation paths, and functional planning are finalized before rendering.

03. Photorealistic 3D Views

High-quality 3D renders with accurate lighting, textures, materials, and depth perception.

04. Revisions & Finalization

Unlimited minor edits until you are fully satisfied and confident to move into execution.
