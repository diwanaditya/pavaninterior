Thoughtfully Designed Modular Kitchens

At Pavan Interior Design, we create modular kitchens that balance aesthetics, efficiency, and durability. Every kitchen is planned around your cooking habits, storage needs, and lifestyle—ensuring seamless movement, optimal space usage, and long-lasting performance.

01. Space Analysis

Precise measurements and layout planning based on utilities and workflow.

02. 3D Visualization

Detailed 3D designs with layout, finishes, colors, and lighting.

03. Customization

Multiple design revisions until the kitchen reflects your preferences.

04. Execution

Factory-finished modules installed with precision and site supervision.

L-Shaped Kitchen

Ideal for compact and medium-sized homes, L-shaped kitchens maximize corner space while keeping the cooking zone efficient and open.

U-Shaped Kitchen

Designed for high storage and workflow efficiency, U-shaped kitchens offer ample counter space and structured organization.

Parallel Kitchen

Perfect for narrow spaces, parallel kitchens ensure smooth movement between cooking and prep zones.

Island & Hybrid Kitchens

A premium choice for larger homes, island kitchens add social and functional value while hybrid designs blend storage and open layouts.
