Wall Panel & Cladding Solutions

Transform plain walls into architectural statements with premium wall panels and cladding solutions designed for elegance, durability, and timeless appeal.

Why Wall Panels & Cladding Matter

Wall panels and cladding are not just decorative elements — they define character, add depth, improve acoustics, and elevate the overall ambience of a space. At Pavan Interior Design, we carefully design wall treatments that complement architecture, furniture, lighting, and lifestyle.

Types of Wall Panels & Cladding We Offer

Wooden Wall Panels

Warm, natural finishes ideal for living rooms, bedrooms, and feature walls.

Stone & Texture Cladding

Natural stone, veneer, and textured surfaces for premium visual impact.

Fluted & 3D Panels

Modern fluted, grooved, and 3D panels for contemporary interiors.

TV Unit Back Panels

Custom-designed TV wall panels integrating storage and lighting.

Commercial Wall Cladding

Durable, low-maintenance wall solutions for offices and retail spaces.

Exterior & Semi-Outdoor Cladding

Weather-resistant cladding materials for balconies and façade accents.
