Architecture Consulting Services

Architecture is the foundation of every successful interior and construction project. At Pavan Interior Design, our architecture consulting services ensure that every space is structurally sound, functionally efficient, legally compliant, and visually balanced before execution begins.

Whether you are planning a residence, office, commercial space, or turnkey development, our architectural team bridges the gap between concept, engineering, and execution — reducing costly errors, delays, and rework.

Why Architectural Consulting Is Essential

Without proper architectural planning, even the best interiors fail in execution. Our consulting ensures correct space planning, circulation flow, natural light optimization, structural coordination, and compliance with local building regulations.

We act as your technical guide — translating ideas into buildable drawings that contractors, engineers, and interior teams can execute accurately.

Our Architectural Consulting Process

Site Study & Requirement Analysis

We evaluate plot conditions, existing structures, bylaws, client needs, and future scalability.

Concept Planning & Space Zoning

Efficient layouts, circulation paths, room proportions, and functional zoning are developed.

Detailed Drawings & Coordination

Working drawings coordinated with structural, electrical, plumbing, and interior layouts.

Execution Support

On-site clarifications, revisions, and coordination with contractors during execution.

Types of Projects We Handle

Our architectural consulting expertise spans across:

  • • Residential villas, apartments, and bungalows
  • • Corporate offices and IT workspaces
  • • Retail stores, showrooms, and commercial complexes
  • • Turnkey interior-integrated architectural projects

Materials, Standards & Compliance

We ensure architectural decisions align with long-term durability, safety, and sustainability. Our consulting considers:

  • Structural grid optimization for cost efficiency
  • Material compatibility with interior finishes
  • Vastu-sensitive planning (where required)
  • Fire safety norms and building regulations
  • Energy-efficient orientation and ventilation
